## 1. Introduction

NextEleven Studios ("we," "our," or "us") operates the B2B Complaint-Driven Product Ideation Application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

**By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.**

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **API Keys**: When you use our Service, you may provide third-party API keys (such as xAI API keys) for authentication purposes. These keys are stored securely in your session and are not persisted beyond your session unless you explicitly configure persistent storage.

- **Usage Data**: We collect information about how you interact with our Service, including:
  - Tools selected for analysis
  - Analysis preferences and settings
  - Export activities
  - Session identifiers

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Scraped Data**: Our Service automatically scrapes publicly available review data from third-party websites (G2.com and Capterra) in accordance with their terms of service. This data includes:
  - Review text and ratings
  - Review dates
  - Tool names and categories
  - Source identifiers

- **Technical Information**: We may collect:
  - IP addresses
  - Browser type and version
  - Operating system
  - Access times and dates
  - Session identifiers

### 2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use session-based storage to maintain your preferences and session state. We do not use persistent cookies for tracking purposes.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1. **Service Provision**: To provide, operate, and maintain our Service
2. **Analysis**: To perform complaint pattern analysis and generate product ideas
3. **Improvement**: To improve, personalize, and expand our Service
4. **Communication**: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
5. **Compliance**: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms
6. **Security**: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats

## 4. Data Storage and Retention

### 4.1 Storage

- **Session Data**: API keys and session preferences are stored in your browser session and are cleared when you close your browser, unless you configure persistent storage.

- **Scraped Reviews**: Publicly scraped review data is stored in our database for caching purposes to improve Service performance and reduce redundant scraping.

- **Analysis Results**: Analysis results may be stored with session identifiers for a limited period to enable result retrieval and Service improvement.

### 4.2 Retention Period

- **Scraped Data**: Retained for up to 90 days (configurable) or until deletion is requested
- **Analysis Results**: Retained for up to 90 days (configurable) or until deletion is requested
- **Session Data**: Cleared when your session ends, unless persistent storage is configured
- **Audit Logs**: Retained as required by law or for security purposes

### 4.3 Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the address provided in Section 10.
